Private device discovery

Know what’s
around you.

There’s usually more smart devices around you than you think: a neighbour’s speaker, a ring, a Wi-Fi plug you forgot about, or a phone that isn’t yours sitting on your Wi-Fi.

LanternScan finds BLE and Wi-Fi devices near you and tells you what it thinks they are. For each device you get a best guess at the make and model, plus the evidence behind that guess so you can decide for yourself whether it makes sense. Anything you recognise, mark as familiar. Anything you don’t, flag it and monitor closely.
